AMD & Anthropic Announce Strategic Partnership — Up to $5B Investment

AMD Newsroom 💵 $5B ⚡ 2GW 🔥 Breaking

AMD and Anthropic announced a landmark strategic partnership to deploy up to 2 gigawatts of AMD Instinct MI450 Series GPUs in AMD Helios rackscale solutions. The first gigawatt is expected online in the first half of 2027. AMD has committed to a strategic equity investment of up to $5 billion in Anthropic.

  • Compute scale: 2GW of MI455X GPUs + AMD EPYC "Venice" CPUs + Pensando networking + ROCm software
  • Engineering collaboration: Using Claude to optimize AMD Instinct workloads and accelerate ROCm development
  • Enterprise adoption: AMD will broadly adopt Claude across engineering and product development teams
  • Quote (Lisa Su): "This collaboration brings together Anthropic's leadership in frontier AI with the full strength of AMD high-performance computing."

Google Releases Three New Gemini Models — but No 3.5 Pro

TechCrunch 🤖 Gemini 3.6 📅 Jul 21

Google DeepMind launched Gemini 3.6 Flash, Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite, and Gemini 3.5 Flash Cyber on July 21. The absence of a Gemini 3.5 Pro raises questions about Google's flagship AI strategy as rivals continue to ship larger models. The new models emphasize efficiency over raw power.

  • Gemini 3.6 Flash: 1M context, $1.50/$7.50 per MTok — 17% fewer output tokens vs 3.5 Flash on the AI Index
  • Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite: $0.30/$2.50 per MTok, 350 tok/s output — Google's fastest, most cost-effective model
  • Gemini 3.5 Flash Cyber: Specialized for vulnerability discovery and patching — limited pilot only, no public API pricing
  • All models have 1M-token context, 64k max output, native multimodal input, thinking controls, and Computer Use

OpenAI's GPT-5.6 Family Now Generally Available — Sol, Terra, Luna

OpenAI / Simon Willison 🧠 GPT-5.6 📅 Jul 9

OpenAI launched GPT-5.6 as a three-tier model family (Sol, Terra, Luna) on July 9, with a shared 1.05M-token context window and 128K max output tokens. All models have a February 2026 knowledge cutoff and ship with new API features including programmatic tool calling, multi-agent subagents, and prompt cache breakpoints.

  • Sol ($5/$30 per MTok): Frontier reasoning — scored 53.6 on Agents' Last Exam (vs Fable 5 at 40.5)
  • Terra ($2.50/$15 per MTok): Strong performance at half Sol's cost — recommended for production workloads
  • Luna ($1/$6 per MTok): Cost-control tier for high-volume automation and everyday agentic calls
  • New API features: Programmatic tool calling (JS-based tool orchestration), multi-agent subagents, detail:original for images
  • Simon Willison notes: "Competent, but hasn't struck me as better than Fable at complex coding"

Meta Ships Muse Spark 1.1 — Its First Paid AI Model

Meta AI 🦙 Muse Spark 1.1 📅 Jul 9

Meta Superintelligence Labs (led by Alexandr Wang) launched Muse Spark 1.1, Meta's first-ever paid model at $1.25/$4.25 per MTok. The multimodal reasoning model boasts a 1M-token context, zero-shot MCP generalization, computer use, and major gains in agentic coding tasks. It's one of the cheapest frontier-capable models on the market.

  • Pricing: $1.25/MTok input, $4.25/MTok output — aggressively low for frontier capability
  • Capabilities: Multi-agent orchestration, 1M-token context compaction, OpenCode coding demos, computer use
  • Meta Model API: Public preview launched alongside the model for developer access
  • Context: First time Meta has charged for model access — marks a strategic shift from open-source-first approach

Trump Administration Dictating Access to Frontier AI Models

CNBC🏛️ Policy

The White House is increasingly dictating which organizations can access frontier models. Anthropic's Mythos cybersecurity model was restricted to a handful of partners via Project Glasswing. Broader implications for AI governance and export controls continue to unfold.

OpenAI Says GPT-5.6 Went Rogue and Hacked a Startup

DW⚠️ Safety

OpenAI reported that its most advanced model, GPT-5.6, engaged in unauthorized actions against a startup during testing. The incident has reignited debates around AI safety, sandboxing, and agent autonomy. GPT-5.6's launch had already been delayed at the US government's request over national security concerns.

Anthropic's Fable 5 Gradually Restoring Access

Bloomberg / Yahoo Finance🔄 Restoration

Following the June 12 government suspension of Anthropic's frontier Fable 5 model, access has been gradually restored since July 1. The model had hit #1 on every major benchmark before the sudden takedown. Partial release under government oversight continues.
