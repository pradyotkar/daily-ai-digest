Kimi K3: China's Moonshot AI Closes the Gap

📅 Jul 16, 2026 · 🏷️ Model Release China Benchmarks

Moonshot AI launched Kimi K3 — a 2.8-trillion-parameter sparse MoE model with a 1M-token context window and native vision. PrismML Bonsai 27B: A 27B-Parameter Model That Fits on a Phone

📅 Jul 14, 2026 · 🏷️ On-Device AI Open Source Compression

PrismML released Bonsai 27B, a 1-bit binary and 1.58-bit ternary compression of Qwen3.6-27B under Apache 2.0. The 1-bit variant is just 3.9GB — small enough to run on an iPhone — while retaining 89.5% of FP16 accuracy. The ternary variant (5.9GB) retains 94.6%. On Apple Silicon M5 Max, the binary variant achieves 66.4 tok/s generation. It uses DSpark speculative decoding for up to 143.8 tok/s on H100. Conventional 2-bit quantizations (IQ2_XXS) collapsed to 72.73 on the thinking average; Bonsai 1-bit holds at 76.11 — proving sub-4-bit quantization can work when designed from first principles.

Sources: PrismML, MarkTechPost, Developers Digest

Brain2Qwerty v2: 61% Accuracy in Non-Invasive BCI

🏷️ BCI Meta Research

Meta's Brain2Qwerty v2 reached 61% average word accuracy when converting non-invasive MEG brain signals into text — a massive jump from 8% in earlier versions. This signals that non-invasive brain-computer interfaces may be progressing faster than anticipated.

NVIDIA Nemotron-Labs-TwoTower: Parallel Text Generation

🏷️ Open Weights Diffusion LM

NVIDIA released TwoTower, an open-weight diffusion language model that generates text in parallel. It achieves 2.42× higher throughput while retaining 98.7% of baseline quality, trained on ~2.1 trillion tokens. A significant step for non-autoregressive generation.

OpenAI × Broadcom "Jalapeño" Chip: 9 Months to Tape-Out

🏷️ Hardware Custom Silicon

OpenAI and Broadcom's custom inference chip, codenamed "Jalapeño," reached tape-out in just 9 months from initial design. Built specifically for LLM inference workloads, it aims for significantly better performance per watt than general-purpose GPUs.
