🚀 OpenAI GPT-5.6 Goes Public — Sol, Terra & Luna Now Available

OpenAI's GPT-5.6 family — initially previewed in a government-gated release — opened to all users on July 9, 2026. The three-model lineup spans Luna (entry), Terra (balanced), and Sol (frontier), all with a 1M token context window and 128K max output tokens. On Agents' Last Exam, GPT-5.6 Sol scored 53.6%, beating Claude Fable 5 by 13.1 points. Sol on Cerebras delivers up to 750 tokens/sec. Luna ($1/$6 per MTok) and Terra ($2.50/$15) both outscore Fable 5 at roughly 1/16th the cost. The public rollout followed a national security review by the Trump administration.
